## Business Overview

Apex Manufacturing Group is a mid-size American industrial manufacturer headquartered in
Chicago, Illinois. Founded in 2002, AMG designs and manufactures industrial components,
precision assemblies, and specialty materials for clients across the automotive, construction,
and industrial equipment sectors.

The company operates 2 production plants, 2 distribution/warehouse facilities, 1 R&D center,
and 1 corporate headquarters across the United States. AMG employs approximately 3,400 people
and generates over $500 million in annual revenue.

**Annual procurement spend exceeds $78 million across 10 spend categories and 30 approved suppliers.**

## Key Analytical Storylines (for SQL analytics phase)

1. **Supplier Concentration Risk** — Top 5 strategic suppliers control ~50% of spend. Pareto analysis will show this clearly.
2. **Late Delivery Pattern** — Approved/Spot suppliers have significantly higher late delivery rates. Useful for risk scoring queries.
3. **Category Spend Trends** — Raw Materials and Industrial Components dominate Direct spend. IT and Professional Services drive Indirect.
4. **Payment Health** — Some suppliers have overdue/disputed invoices. Aging analysis demonstrates business value.
5. **Location Spend Distribution** — Houston and Detroit Plants account for majority of direct material spend.
6. **Supplier Performance Deterioration** — Suppliers 20, 24, and 26 show declining on-time delivery over the 3-year period (baked into the data intentionally for trend analysis).
